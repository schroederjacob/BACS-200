J.R.R. Tolkien

Authors can often be some of the most inspirational people throughout history. They create stories that can spark the imagination, or open discussion on some of the social issues of our time.

Over the last 100 years, I very few authors have been more influential than J.R.R. Tolkien.

Tolkien's tales of heroes and friendship have inspired countless people, from musicians and authors, to the everyday readers, to even soldiers in trenches of WWII.
