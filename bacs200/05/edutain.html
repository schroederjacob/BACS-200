How Humans Broke the Game

  • High intelligence enabled us to create advanced tools.
  • Our physical builds and the ability to sweat made us excellent long-distance hunters.
  • A bipedal stance freed up our hands for carrying food, which encouraged a very communal lifestyle that helped us grow.

Are Insects Getting Banned?

  • Many highly specialized insect species such as the Monarch butterfly are losing their primary food sources due to habitat destruction.
  • Aquatic insect species are being affected by the rise in pollution.
  • Some species, such as cockroaches, are actually thriving in this changing world.
